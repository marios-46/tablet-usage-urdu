Basic Types of Tablets

Swallowed with water for general use.

Chewable Tablets

Chewed before swallowing.

Effervescent Tablets

Dissolved in water before use.

Coated Tablets

Designed for easier swallowing and protection.

Understanding Dosage and Instructions

Dosage is one of the most important parts of medicine usage. Taking too much or too little can affect results. Always follow the instructions carefully.

  • Check how many times a day to take the tablet
  • Follow timing instructions (before/after meals)
  • Use proper measurements when needed
  • Do not change dosage without proper guidance
